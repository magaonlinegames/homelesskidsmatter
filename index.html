We are a nonprofit fighting child poverty, disease, and inequity around the world. We work in the hardest-to-reach places, where it’s toughest to be a child. When you give to Homeless Kids Matter, 85% of every dollar goes straight to our mission to provide lifesaving relief to children.

Emergency Alert.
Russia-Ukraine War.

As hundreds of thousands of Ukrainians escape to safety, many more are left behind. The most vulnerable have no options for escape. Disabled children are trapped as Russian troops invade.

Days of sheltering as air raid sirens scream to stay indoors mean many families face food and essential supplies shortages. People who can go to stores wait in hours-long queues. For families with disabled children, the burden is intensified.

Working together, we invest in the lives of children and youth, build the healthy environments they need to thrive, and empower them to create lasting change in their own lives and communities.
